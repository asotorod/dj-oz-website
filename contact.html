FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How far in advance should I book?
    We recommend booking 6-12 months in advance for weddings and 2-3 months for other events.
  • Do you require a deposit?
    Yes, a deposit is required to secure your date. The remaining balance is due before the event.
  • Can I provide my own music playlist?
    Absolutely! We work with your playlist and can also integrate Spotify or Apple Music.
  • Do you travel outside the Tri-State area?
    Yes! We're available for destination events. Travel fees may apply.
  • What if I need to change my date?
    We understand plans change. Contact us as soon as possible and we'll work with you.
  • Do you have backup equipment?
    Yes, we always bring backup equipment to ensure your event runs smoothly.
